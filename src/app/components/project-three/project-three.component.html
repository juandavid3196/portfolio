Quiz App

Welcome to an interactive knowledge challenge, customize your experience by selecting the number of questions, categories, and difficulty levels. Test your general knowledge and explore a variety of topics while having fun and learning fascinating facts. Perfect for trivia enthusiasts and curious minds alike!

Authentication system

Users can register and log in to personalize their quiz experience and track their progress.

Quiz Customization Menu

Having the option to customize their quiz by selecting the number of questions, categories, difficulty levels, and setting a time limit for each quiz.

Scoring System

Points are awarded based on the accuracy and speed of answers, providing instant feedback at the end of the quiz

Ranking Page

A leaderboard where users can view their ranking and compare their scores with other players globally, fostering competition and motivation to improve.

Firebase connected

The app is integrated with Firestore for real-time data management, while Firebase Authentication securely handles user login and registration. User profiles, quiz settings, and scores are stored and updated instantly, providing a seamless and personalized experience.
